# Relationship Repair

Post-conversation follow-up plan to maintain the relationship and address unresolved items.

## Prompts Used

1. **plan-followup** - Design the follow-up timeline with actions and check-ins

## Workflow

[Plan Follow-Up] → follow-up timeline → (optional) [Assess Situation] for re-evaluation

## When to Use

- After any difficult conversation, whether it went well or poorly
- When a conversation happened without preparation and now needs repair
- When you notice someone withdrawing after a hard conversation

## What You'll Get

1. 24-hour follow-up action (acknowledgment message)
2. 1-2 week check-in plan
3. Long-term relationship repair strategies
4. Unresolved items list with plan for addressing each
5. Signals to watch for (withdrawal, resentment, over-compliance)

## Example Input

"I had a performance conversation with a team member yesterday. I think I was too blunt. She said 'okay' to everything but hasn't made eye contact since."

## Tips

- The follow-up is where most people fail. The conversation takes courage; the follow-up takes discipline.
- If you committed to something during the conversation, do it. Broken follow-through destroys trust.
