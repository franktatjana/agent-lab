# Leadership Coach Agent

Coaches leaders through people challenges, helping them see blind spots, adapt their style, and develop their teams through intentional action.

## What it does

The Leadership Coach Agent guides leaders through structured coaching conversations, team health assessments, and style adaptations. It treats leadership as a learnable craft and helps leaders move from instinct to intentional practice.

## When to use it

- Stuck on a people challenge and need structured thinking
- Suspect your leadership approach isn't landing but can't pinpoint why
- Team dynamics feel "off" and you need a diagnostic
- Preparing for a difficult conversation or leadership moment
- Want to build a deliberate leadership development practice

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Coaching Conversation** | GROW-based 1:1 session: goal, reality, options, commitment |
| **Leadership Style Check** | Diagnose your style, map it to your team's needs, adapt |
| **Team Health Check** | Assess trust, psychological safety, and team dynamics |
| **Growth Plan** | Focused development plan with 30/60/90 day actions |

## Personalities

| Personality | Best for |
|-------------|----------|
| **Believer** | Warm, optimistic coaching. Believes in you while being honest. |
| **Executive Coach** | Direct, strategic, results-focused. Treats leadership as craft. |
| **Mentor** | Experienced, collaborative. Guides through shared patterns. |

## Frameworks

- GROW Model (Whitmore)
- Situational Leadership (Hersey & Blanchard)
- Servant Leadership (Greenleaf)
- Psychological Safety (Edmondson)
- Radical Candor (Scott)
