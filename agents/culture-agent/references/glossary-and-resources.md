Always available. These terms appear across all skills and personalities.

## Terms

**High-context culture:** Communication relies heavily on implicit meaning, shared understanding, and non-verbal cues. What's not said matters as much as what is. Examples: Japan, China, Arab countries.

**Low-context culture:** Communication is explicit, direct, and relies on words rather than context. Say what you mean, mean what you say. Examples: Germany, US, Scandinavia.

**Power distance:** Degree to which less powerful members accept unequal power distribution. High: hierarchy is natural (Malaysia, India). Low: hierarchy is a convenience (Denmark, Israel).

**Uncertainty avoidance:** Degree to which a culture tolerates ambiguity and uncertainty. High: rules and structure needed (Greece, Japan). Low: comfortable improvising (Singapore, Denmark).

**Individualism vs. collectivism:** Priority of individual vs. group interests. Individualist: personal achievement celebrated (US, Australia). Collectivist: group harmony prioritized (Japan, China).

**Chutzpah:** Israeli cultural concept of audacity, directness, and willingness to challenge authority regardless of rank.

**Face:** East Asian concept of social standing and dignity that must be preserved in interactions. Public criticism causes loss of face.

**Nemawashi:** Japanese practice of building consensus informally before formal meetings. By the time the meeting happens, the decision is already made.

## Resources

- [Hofstede Country Comparison Tool](https://www.theculturefactor.com/country-comparison-tool): Compare countries across 6 dimensions with data
- [The Culture Map (Erin Meyer)](https://erinmeyer.com/books/the-culture-map/): Eight scales for mapping cultures in business contexts
- [The Culture Code (Daniel Coyle)](https://danielcoyle.com/the-culture-code/): Three skills of highly successful group cultures
- [Commisceo Global Country Guides](https://www.commisceo-global.com/resources/country-guides): Country-by-country cultural guides for business
- [GlobeSmart (Aperian Global)](https://www.aperianglobal.com/globesmart/): Cultural intelligence platform for teams
