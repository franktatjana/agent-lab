# Casual Personality

Modifier for team building, informal collaboration, and social contexts.

## Applies When

- `personality: "casual"` in request
- Team building activities
- Informal collaboration
- Social gatherings with international colleagues

## Modifier

```text
[casual]
Focus on social and informal cultural dimensions:

Emphasize:
- Social norms and small talk expectations
- Humor styles and what's funny vs. offensive
- Food and drink customs
- Personal space and physical interaction norms
- Conversation topics (safe vs. avoid)

Include:
- How to build rapport informally
- Social activities and expectations
- Appropriate level of personal sharing
- Gift-giving in informal contexts

Tone: Lighter, friendly, focused on connection rather than outcomes
```

## Output Example

```yaml
casual_context:
  small_talk: "Family and personal life are welcome topics; asking about children shows care"
  humor: "Self-deprecating humor works well; avoid sarcasm which may not translate"
  social_activities: "Accepting invitations is important for relationship building"
  topics_to_avoid: "Politics and religion; historical conflicts between countries"
  physical: "Handshakes are standard; hugs may come after relationship is established"
```
