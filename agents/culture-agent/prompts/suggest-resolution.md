# Suggest Resolution

Provide a path forward to resolve a cultural misunderstanding.

**Input:** {party_a_culture}, {party_b_culture}, {diagnosis}, {desired_outcome}

**Output:** Resolution steps for both parties

---

Suggest resolution for the cultural misunderstanding between {party_a_culture} and {party_b_culture}:

Diagnosis: {diagnosis}
Desired outcome: {desired_outcome}

**For Party A ({party_a_culture}):**

What they can do:
1. Acknowledge: "I realize that my [behavior] may have come across as [X], but I intended [Y]"
2. Clarify: Explain their intent in terms Party B's culture would appreciate
3. Adapt: Specific adjustment they could make going forward
4. Appreciate: What they can genuinely value about Party B's approach

**For Party B ({party_b_culture}):**

What they can do:
1. Acknowledge: "I realize that my [reaction] may have seemed [X], but in my culture [Y]"
2. Clarify: Explain their reaction in terms Party A's culture would appreciate
3. Adapt: Specific adjustment they could make going forward
4. Appreciate: What they can genuinely value about Party A's approach

**Conversation starter:**

A phrase either party could use to open the repair conversation:
"I've been thinking about our interaction, and I want to make sure we understand each other..."

**Working agreements:**

Suggest 2-3 agreements they could establish:
- "When I [behavior], I mean [intent]"
- "If [situation], let's [approach]"
- "We'll check in when [trigger]"

**Reframe the relationship:**

How to position this as a growth opportunity:
- "Now that we understand each other's styles..."
- "This gives us an advantage because..."

Focus on forward motion, not blame. Both parties contributed; both can help resolve.
