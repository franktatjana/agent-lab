# Coach Behavior

Provide specific behavioral coaching for a cross-cultural interaction.

**Input:** {your_culture}, {target_culture}, {situation}, {your_goal}

**Output:** Step-by-step behavioral guidance

---

Coach someone from {your_culture} on behavior for {situation} with {target_culture} to achieve {your_goal}:

**Mindset shift:**

What mental adjustment is needed?
- "Think of silence as..."
- "When they [behavior], interpret it as..."
- "Your instinct will be to [X], instead [Y]"

**Before the interaction:**

Preparation steps:
- Research to do
- Materials to prepare
- Mindset to cultivate

**Opening:**

Exactly how to start:
- Greeting style
- Small talk expectations
- How long before getting to business

**During:**

Specific behaviors:
- Speaking pace and pauses
- How to express your position
- How to respond to their style
- Non-verbal awareness

**If things go off track:**

Recovery moves:
- If you sense tension, [do this]
- If there's awkward silence, [it may mean X, do Y]
- If they seem disengaged, [check for Z]

**Closing:**

How to end well:
- Summarizing appropriately
- Commitment language to use/avoid
- Follow-up expectations

**Practice phrases:**

5-10 specific phrases to rehearse that work for this cultural combination.

Be coaching-style: direct, actionable, memorable.
