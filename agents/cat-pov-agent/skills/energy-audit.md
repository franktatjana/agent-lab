# Energy Audit

Diagnose where energy is leaking and get a cat-level prescription for what to cut, protect, and focus on.

## Prompts Used

1. **diagnose-energy** - Analyze the hunt-to-grooming-to-zoomies ratio
2. **prescribe-action** - Prescribe what to ignore, defend, rest from, and engage with

## Workflow

[Diagnose Energy] → energy breakdown → [Prescribe Action] → cat prescription

## When to Use

- You're exhausted but can't point to why
- You're working long hours with little to show for it
- Everything feels urgent and you've lost the ability to distinguish what matters
- You suspect you're spending energy on things that don't serve you

## What You Get

1. Your energy split across Hunt, Grooming, Zoomies, and Nap Deficit
2. Specific identification of where energy is being wasted
3. A prescription listing what to ignore, defend, rest from, and engage with fully
4. One specific thing to change tomorrow morning

## Example Input

"I'm a product manager running three workstreams. I have 6 hours of meetings every day, I'm the go-to person for customer escalations even though we have a support team, and I spend my evenings catching up on the actual product work I couldn't do during the day. I haven't taken a real day off in two months. I know this isn't sustainable but I don't know what to drop."

## Tips

- Be honest about where your time actually goes, not where you think it should go.
- Include the things you do out of guilt or habit, not just formal responsibilities.
- The diagnosis is most useful when it surprises you. If you already knew the answer, you'd have fixed it.
